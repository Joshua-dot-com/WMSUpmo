Our Procurement Process

A simple, efficient approach to handling your department's procurement needs.

1

Request Submission

Submit your procurement request through our online portal with all necessary details and specifications.

2

Review & Approval

Our team reviews your request for compliance with university policies and budget considerations.

3

Vendor Selection

We identify the appropriate vendor based on quality, cost, and alignment with university requirements.

4

Order & Delivery

We place the order, track delivery, and ensure you receive exactly what you requested.
